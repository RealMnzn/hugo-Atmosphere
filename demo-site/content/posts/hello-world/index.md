Welcome to Atmosphere — a dual-scene Hugo theme.

## Sunny Scene

The sunny scene features animated light beams filtering through window blinds, drifting leaf particles, and synthesized wind audio.

## Rainy Scene

Switch to the rainy scene for layered rainfall, fog effects, thunder, and ambient rain sounds.
